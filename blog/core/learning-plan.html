Bootcamp learning plan

What is your long term goal and/or career pathway?

Working as a software developer on projects that are making the world a better place, in a place that has opportunities for growth, that pays me well, ideally a company that promotes an environment of candour and trust across the whole company. Leadership.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

Strengths

  • Knowing JS well, experience with React, & have touched some backend tech
  • Coding problem solving skills
  • Communication skills
  • Habit of meditation & exercise
  • Committed to learning

I think that this set of strengths will serve me really well to learn and work well with others during Bootcamp.

Limitations

  • Overthinking things
  • Having to find some balance between bootcamp & family
  • Leaving it too long to ask for help
  • Making sure I read the instructions properly

It’s important to have a good balance between study & family for everyone's wellbeing. If I overthink my blog posts etc. during boot camp then it could cause me to fall behind, or just get stressed. Obviously it’s important to ask for help in a timely fashion, I am definitely getting better at this but want to keep it in mind so I don’t fall back into old habits. Making sure that you read the instructions properly reduces wasted time. These are all things that I should be mindful of.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

Staying focused for so many hours over 9 weeks, but also switching off during downtime.

I have really noticed over the foundations course that it has taken a bit of time for me to let the problem solving go once the day is done, especially if I have an unsolved problem that I started, so switching off to make sure I don’t burn out will be a big one. Which should help facilitate turning up to do the extra work in evenings and on the weekend.

A commitment to how you will manage your workload in this programme. This should include:

I will communicate what I can do, what I can’t do and what I don’t think I can do.

I will take ownership of what I say I’m going to do

I will do what is expected of me to the best of my ability. If I’m falling short for whatever reason, I will assess whether I am putting in enough effort, & communicate promptly with the appropriate people, and see what kind of resolution we can come up with.

If things are building up and starting to get to me, first I will write about whatever is happening that’s getting to me. If that’s not enough to solve the problem I will reach out to someone as soon as possible to express my feelings, if there is still no resolution, I will keep reaching out to different people.

A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way

If I am stuck or having a problem, I will first seek help in the appropriate channels i.e. “tech questions”. If it’s really negatively affecting my learning & I haven’t had a quick enough response, I will quickly escalate the problem to facilitators. I will provide specific details about the problem, what I’ve tried to solve it, and what I’m looking at.
