Te Whare Tapa Whā - Wellbeing plan

image from takai.nz / CC BY 4.0

This is my wellbeing plan for the next 3 months+ studying with Dev Academy.

Taha Tinana (Physical Wellbeing)

Yoga 2x per week during lunch breaks.

30 min hike 3x per week during lunch breaks.

Bite size exercise breaks during the 5 minute pomodoro timer breaks.

Sit/stand & kneel at my sit/stand desk.

8 hours of sleep a night.

Taha Whānau (Family Wellbeing)

Be at the table for family dinner & breakfast every night.

Be fully present with my family when we are together.

Keep in touch with friends online during downtime.

Be fully present with everyone at Dev Academy when I’m with them.

Spend some time with whanau and local friends on the weekends.

Have a plan about study/life balance & communicate it effectively with everyone affected.

Taha Hinengaro (Emotional and Mental Wellbeing)

Eat well, continue to have some good salads with my protein.

Meditate 3 mornings a week, and do a mindful walking session 2 other mornings.

Communicate with the people around me.

Keep focussing on long relaxing exhales.

Take time out to let go of everything 2x a week with 20 mins of NSDR or yin yoga

Taha Wairua (Spiritual Wellbeing)

Do my gratitude practice every evening.

Meditate 3 mornings a week, and do a mindful walking session 2 other mornings.

Continue learning german.

Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment)

Keep walking up the hill 3x a week.

Go on evening walks with the family and look at the stars.

Water my houseplants and check they’re okay.

Tend the garden for little stints, to pull weeds, water plants, mow lawns.

Listen to the birds.

Look at the weather and feel it.
